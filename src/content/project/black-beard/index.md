## ğŸ‰ What is Black Beard?

**Black Beard** is an Android app designed to help bearded dragon owners care for their scaly companions with confidence. Owning an exotic pet comes with a steep learning curve â€” from setting up the perfect enclosure to understanding common behaviors. Black Beard puts trusted care knowledge in your pocket and helps you stay organized.

Whether you're a first-time reptile owner or a seasoned keeper, Black Beard simplifies exotic pet care and daily routines.

## ğŸ’¡ Why Black Beard?

The reptile community has always been incredibly generous, sharing knowledge through blogs, forums, and videos. Black Beard was created to give back by consolidating trusted information into one easy-to-use app.

All guidance in the app comes from extensive research and personal experience caring for bearded dragons, using sources long trusted by the community.
